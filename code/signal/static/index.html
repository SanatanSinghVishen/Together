together.signal

Know how your startups are doing in real time.

Signal reviews weekly progress updates from portfolio founders to alert partners about performance drops, blocker issues, or founder burnout.

How It Works

01

Founder Logins

Founders write a simple, short weekly text update about their metrics, challenges, and stress levels.

02

Automatic Reviews

The system reads the updates to pull out revenue trends, active blockers, and sentiment shifts, classifying the startup status in real time.

03

Alert Prioritization

If a startup is facing revenue drops or high founder stress, it rises to the top of the triage board, alerting partners immediately.

Strategic Value

Early Intervention

Spot startup trouble (blockers or metric drops) weeks before it shows up in official board meetings.

Founder Care

Check in on founder sentiment and burnout markers to offer support when they need it most.
